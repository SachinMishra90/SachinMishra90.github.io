Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Digital Office Management ("hum", "humara", ya "website") mein aapka swagat hai. Hum aapki privacy ka poora sammaan karte hain. Ye policy batati hai ki hum user ka data kaise handle karte hain.

1. No Data Collection (User Privacy)

Hamari website ek "Client-Side" utility platform hai. Iska matlab hai ki jab aap PDF compress ya merge karte hain, toh wo processing aapke apne browser (Mobile/Laptop) mein hoti hai. Hum aapki koi bhi file hamare server par upload nahi karte aur na hi save karte hain.

2. Cookies aur Third-Party Ads

Hum apni website par Google AdSense ka use karte hain ads dikhane ke liye. Google, "cookies" ka use kar sakta hai taaki wo aapki interest ke mutabik ads dikha sake. Aap apne browser ki settings mein jakar cookies ko disable kar sakte hain.

3. Log Files

Jaise har standard website karti hai, hum basic information jaise IP address, browser type, aur visit ka samay monitor karte hain sirf traffic analysis ke liye. Isse aapki personal identity leak nahi hoti.

4. Bachon ki Privacy

Hum 13 saal se kam umar ke bachon se jaan-boojh kar koi personal information collect nahi karte.

5. Consent

Hamari website use karke aap hamari Privacy Policy se sahmat hote hain.
